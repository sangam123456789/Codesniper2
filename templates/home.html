About the Content of website


First of all I welcome you and hope you are doing great. The motive of this website is to provide you with the modern templates being used and coding question which are mostly not standard from different resources. In the Templates section I have provided Links to various templates like Disjoint Set Union , Segment Tree , Lowest Common Ancestor , Prime Number Finder , Djikstra shortest path , Prims minimum spanning tree.

Pattern of arrangement


In the Starters section I have provided topics which are required for the basic understanding of code flow. Brain sharpeners include simple short thinking problem to increase the thinking process. Begginers are some standard data structure based problems. In the Intermediate section the topics included are mostly based on the implementation and variety of use of various data structures like vector , stacks , sets , hashmaps , pair etc. In the Advance section the topics are mostly focused on learning some advance ways to solve and visualize the problems. These are sometimes confusing but always interesting to solve! I have tried my best to arrange the problems in the increasing order of difficulty. Always remember that if you are stuck in some problem or solved a problem forcefully please look into others people submission you will definetily learn to code more efficiently.
